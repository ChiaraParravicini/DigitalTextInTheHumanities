About this project

This project takes inspiration from Vera Gheno's "Parole d'altro genere" (2023) ... [ADD CONTENT]

Words of Gender is the prototype of an archive that could be potentially expanded. There is a general lack of awareness regarding female authors. Indeed, a study published by PLOS reports that "emerging evidence demonstrates that female-authored publications are not well represented in course readings in some fields, resulting in a syllabi gender gap."
